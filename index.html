Our Core Services

Home ICU Setup

Advanced ICU equipment installed at home — monitors, oxygen concentrators, ventilators, and more.

Critical Care Nursing

24x7 trained ICU nurses for monitoring, medication, wound dressing, and emergency support.

Doctor on Call

Qualified doctors for home visits & emergency consultations — general physicians & specialists.

Home Physiotherapy

Recovery-focused physiotherapy for stroke, paralysis, orthopedic injuries, and post-surgical rehab.

Elder Care & Companionship

Daily support for seniors — hygiene, meals, companionship, reminders, and emotional care.

Dementia & Alzheimer's Support

Specialized care programs for memory loss, cognitive decline, and behavioral management.

Cancer & Palliative Care

Pain management, nutrition, emotional support, and end-of-life care at home for cancer patients.

Disability & Post-Hospital Care

Mobility help, therapy, hygiene assistance, and home adaptation for individuals with challenges.

Home Nursing Services

Round-the-clock nursing services for elderly, pediatric, maternity, and chronic illness care.

Baby Care & Postnatal Support

Professional help for newborns & mothers — feeding, bathing, massage, sleep cycles, and recovery.

Psychotherapy & Counseling

Mental wellness support from psychologists for depression, anxiety, caregiver fatigue, and more.

Diagnostics & Lab Tests

At-home sample collection for blood tests, ECGs, X-rays, and full-body checkups.

Ambulance & Emergency Services

Quick-response ambulance support, oxygen supply, and hospital transfer coordination.
